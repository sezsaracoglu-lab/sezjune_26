Section 03 / The Practice

Three movements

i. Remember
Train the system
I am training my nervous system to feel safe, whole, and certain before external confirmation. My anxiety is not doubt. It is withdrawal from a familiar emotional state.
"I act as if the woman who would experience that event already exists."
ii. Breathe
Shift the state
Spine upright, chin tucked, shoulders soft. Slow inhale through the nose. Belly, ribs, chest expand. Gentle pause. Slow release through the mouth. Five to ten breaths shift the state.
"Up, and settling. From survival to coherence."
iii. Act
Move from worth
Calm instead of urgency. Groundedness instead of scanning. Self-trust instead of reassurance-seeking. Selective action instead of over-giving. Before any decision: does this honour my worth?
"If it requires me to shrink, it is not worthy."
Section 04 / The Shift

From old story to new truth.

The Old Story
  • I have to chase opportunities.
  • I must work harder than everyone.
  • I have to prove I deserve this.
  • If I slow down, I will miss out.
  • I adjust myself to be accepted.
  • I worry what others think of me.
  • There is not enough for me.
The New Truth
  • Good things come to me easily.
  • I succeed without exhausting myself.
  • I am already good enough.
  • I can slow down and still move forward.
  • I am true to myself, and respected.
  • I respect myself. People respect me.
  • What is meant for me, meets me.
